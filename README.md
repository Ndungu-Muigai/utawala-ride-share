# Utawala Ride Share

Connecting commuters with drivers heading the same way.

## About

Utawala Ride Share is an upcoming platform that bridges the gap between commuters and everyday drivers with personal cars who are already headed in the same direction. Instead of relying on dedicated taxi services, we make it easy to share rides, split costs, and reduce the number of cars on the road.

## Our Mission

To make daily commuting more affordable, efficient, and environmentally friendly by unlocking the potential of empty seats in personal vehicles traveling the same routes.

## What We Are Building

- **Smart Matching** — Connect commuters with drivers already traveling their route.
- **Cost Sharing** — Split fuel and toll costs fairly between riders and drivers.
- **Trust & Safety** — Verified profiles, ratings, and real-time trip tracking for peace of mind.
- **Community** — Build a network of everyday commuters helping each other get around.
- **Greener Cities** — Fewer cars on the road means less congestion and lower emissions.

## How It Works

1. **Drivers** offer their upcoming trip details — where they are going and when.
2. **Commuters** search for rides along their route and request a seat.
3. Both parties confirm, share the ride, and split the cost.
